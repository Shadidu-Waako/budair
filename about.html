About Budair Aid Foundation

Zakat

Our Zakat program collects donations from those who can give and distributes them to those in need. Zakat is a form of giving in Islam to help people who are less fortunate. Through this program, we aim to support individuals and families facing financial difficulties, providing them with essential resources to improve their lives and achieve stability

Our Story

Our journey began with a vision to uplift and empower Ugandan people communitу. Through the dedication of our founders and volunteers, we have been able to make a meaningful impact in the lives of many, providing access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities.

Нашата история

Нашето пътуване започна с визия за издигане и овластяване на общността на хората от Уганда. Чрез отдадеността на нашите основатели и доброволци успяхме да окажем значимо влияние в живота на много хора, осигурявайки достъп до образование, здравеопазване и основни нужди.

Hikayemiz

Yolculuğumuz Uganda halkının topluluğunu yükseltme ve güçlendirme vizyonuyla başladı. Kurucularımızın ve gönüllülerimizin özverisi sayesinde eğitime, sağlık hizmetlerine ve temel ihtiyaçlara erişim sağlayarak birçok kişinin hayatında anlamlı bir etki yaratmayı başardık.
