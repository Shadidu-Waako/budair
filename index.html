About Budair Aid Foundation

Our Story

Our journey began with a vision to uplift and empower Ugandan Muslim communities. Through the dedication of our founders and volunteers, we have been able to make a meaningful impact in the lives of many, providing access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities.

Zakat

Our Zakat program collects donations from those who can give and distributes them to those in need. Zakat is a form of giving in Islam to help people who are less fortunate. Through this program, we aim to support individuals and families facing financial difficulties, providing them with essential resources to improve their lives and achieve stability
